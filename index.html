Who We Are

About Sevenergy

Sevenergy is a UK-based energy solutions company specialising in the wholesale supply of residential energy storage systems and inverters. We partner with distributors, installers, and trade partners to deliver competitively priced, certified products that meet real market demand.

While residential storage remains at our core, our vision extends further. We are building towards a broader energy future — expanding into commercial and industrial storage systems, solar PV, and large-scale energy infrastructure, with the ambition to offer end-to-end consultancy that helps businesses reduce energy costs and achieve sustainability goals.

At Sevenergy, we combine sourcing capability, market insight, and a partner-first approach to support the UK and Europe's transition to smarter, cleaner energy.
